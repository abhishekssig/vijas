Differential Pressure transmitter
The DP transmitter operates based on the principle that as the level of a liquid, gas, or vapor changes in a container or pipeline, it causes a difference in pressure between two points.
Differential Pressure transmitter
The DP transmitter operates based on the principle that as the level of a liquid, gas, or vapor changes in a container or pipeline, it causes a difference in pressure between two points. This pressure difference is then detected by the DP transmitter, which converts the information into an electronic signal.
