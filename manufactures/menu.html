OUR FEATURE PRODUCTS

RF Capacitance Level Switch

It is designed to provide accurate and reliable point level (High and Low Level) information of Solids (Powders and Lumps), Liquids and Slurry stored in vessels and tanks. The electronics consist of an oscillator,adetector and an output relay which is controlled by the detector.

Conductivity Level Switch

Conductivity Level Swith is a simple, low cost, point level measurement switch designed for level detection of conductive liquids having low densities, high viscosities, containing solid particles and to detect interface between non-conductive and conductive liquids.

Vibrating Rod And Fork Type Level Switch

Piezoelectric driven vibration level switch detects high, mid and low levels in vessels with powders and bulk solids and suitable for wide range of applications.

Water Flowmeter

FLOWTECH Make Electromagnetic Meter are intended for measuring flow of electrically conductive liquids in closed pipe lines where the magnetic flux permeates the entire cross sectional area of the liquids flow.
